What is Ember Alert?

Ember Alert tracks NASA satellite data for active fires happening around the world. Our interactive map allows you to check if there are any fires near you. But that's not all! We allow you to submit your geolocation data to us along with a phone number or an email address so that we can notify you if we ever detect a fire close by.

Or... if you ever spot a fire near you, snap a picture and send it to us. We'll match your geolocation data with our active fire coordinates and tag your picture(s) to it!

We want to make the world a safer place by sending out... Ember Alerts.

Who made Ember Alert?

Ember Alert was created at the 2017 Space Apps Challenge in Houston, TX. Seven brilliant individuals, with varying levels of experience in software development or data visualization, collaborated to develop this site in less than 48 hours!

From left-to-right and top-to-bottom: Eric MacEwen, Adrian Nip, Tanweer Rajwani, Paul McLaughlin, Kim Andrew, Sam MacEwen, Caroline Dikibo

What started off as a pipe dream ended up winning the 2017 Space Apps Challenge Houston People's Choice Award!
